    Your streamline scheduling software

    Focus on your business, not the admin.

    No more pesky paperwork

    By recreating and optimizing your existing worksheets in the ServiceStacks app, you will no longer need worry about posting, printing and scanning reams of paper. Reducing your admin workload to allow both you and your team to concentrate on what's important. Giving you more time to focus on your business.

    Tech without getting technical

    Not everyone loves computers, but at ServiceStacks its what we do! We provide a service not just a system, working with you every step of the way. Using our tried and tested methodology we ensure you get up and running without a fuss.

    More ways you benefit.

    Service Schedule

    Keep track of all upcoming work; scheduled, additional or one-off tasks. Using the simple drag and drop functionality allows you to allocate work by client, date or engineer providing a complete view of planned works.

    Inventory Management

    Manage your fire alarms, extinguishers, dry risers and more in one place. Digitally and delivered directly to your field staff's tablets, enabling real-time updates and amendments at each visit.

    Custom Templates

    Display worksheets, certificates and reports on customised templates, tailored to differenet order types, to match you and your client's needs.

    Document Storage

    Store all of your site and order documents in on easily searchable system. enabling easy access of past information such as inventories, device lists, service sheets by the field and office based staff.

    Shared Calendars

    A single view of all works; planned, in progress and completed. Events are updated as field based staff complete each allocated job enabling real-time view of progress made and ability to re-allocate as required.

    Workload Management

    Comprehensive reporting gives you the opportunity to maximise the productivity of your field teams, becoming proactive rather than reactive in understanding and managing workloads.

    Full Testimonial

    Historically we have been a business heavily dependent on a paper based workflow, with engineers manually completing paper dockets to capture works on our client sites. This created a number of challenges for us; delays in return of job sheets, issues around legibility of dockets and the inability to capture a real-time view of those jobs in progress or completed.

    From an initial, conceptual idea, we worked with Ian in developing a mobile software application enabling us to resolve these challenges. The project has been hassle free - Ian has both listened and understood from the beginning, our desire to implement a straight-forward solution which didn't disrupt the way our team worked but enhanced the workflow by enabling us to track the work more easily and to ultimately move towards the completion and invoicing of jobs more quickly.

    Introduction of the app within our business has been a complete game changer. We have been able to replicate and streamline the workflow process for engineers meaning it has been easily accepted in the field. Our Contracts Manager is able to identify real-time progress and proactively change schedules where required and clients now receive a professional pdf report on the back of the visit. Furthermore, time to invoice has been significantly reduced.

    It has been an absolute pleasure working with Ian. His enthusiasm and determination to understand our exact requirements and his "can-do attitude" has ensured that we have a mobile software app that has delivered and met all our criteria. We cannot recommend him highly enough.
